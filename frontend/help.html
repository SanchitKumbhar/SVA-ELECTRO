Frequently Asked Questions

Here you'll find answers to common questions about our electric vehicle technology, switching circuits, and how to connect with our team.

How can I contact the sales team for product inquiries?

You can reach our sales team through the contact page, or directly via WhatsApp, LinkedIn, or Instagram. We aim to respond within 24 hours.

What products does Swa Electro provide?

We offer EV solutions such as battery switching circuits, e-buses, mini transport vehicles, and IoT-powered mobility tech for smart cities.

Is registration required to access services?

Yes, users must register under one of the three categories—Government, Private Organization, or Customer—to gain access to specific features.

How do I get technical support?

Use our support form on the Contact page or initiate a live chat using the WhatsApp button on the lower corner of the website.

Do you offer customizable EV systems?

Yes, all solutions are customizable based on route, load, fleet size, and government/private use-case requirements.
